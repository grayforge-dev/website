Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

DashLog records only what matters — verified GPS data. No driving score hype, no AI guesses.

🏠

Your drives at a glance

Total distance, weekly activity, and recent drives in one clean view. Open the app and instantly see how much you've driven and where you went last.

📋

Trips, neatly logged

Browse every trip by date with distance, time, speed, and route previews. Tap any entry to dive into the full details and route map.

📡

Live tracking, built for driving

Keep speed, GPS status, distance, and direction visible while you drive. Large digits, screen stay-awake, and a compass dial — designed to glance and go.

🗺️

Every route, clearly saved

Review your route, duration, average speed, and key drive stats. Each trip becomes a complete record — generated automatically the moment you stop.

⏯️

Replay the road

Watch the route again with speed changes shown along the way. A timeline scrubber lets you jump to any moment of the drive — perfect for reviewing scenic routes or tough sections.

📊

Your drive patterns

Weekly, monthly, and all-time stats help you understand every drive. Spot trends, set records, and watch your kilometers add up over time.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

No setup, no account. Open the app and go.

1

Tap Start

Hit the big button on the home screen. GPS logging begins immediately and the screen stays awake.

2

Drive Normally

Speed, route, and stats update live on your dashboard. No interaction needed while driving.

3

Stop & Review

Tap stop. A full summary card with map, stats, and speed chart is saved to your logbook instantly.
